About Alex

Hi! Thanks for checking out my page. I'm Alex, a software engineer based in the SF Bay Area. I received my bachelor's in Materials Engineering from UC San Diego and worked in a lab before moving to San Francisco. This lead me to Cruise Automation, a self driving car company where I played a large role in scaling a team of 5 to a testing fleet of about 100. This inspired me to pursue software development through an intensive software engineering program. I have since continued to sharpen my skills in Javascript, React, Redux, Ruby on Rails, amongst others.

I thrive in fast-changing environments and am no stranger to having to quickly respond to unexpected problems. I'm always seeking to work on cool and impactful projects with awesome people, so I would love to hear how I can make an impact with you or your organization!

woofblr

Woof! woofblr is a full-stack web-application clone of Tumblr, except for dogs! The application utilizes Ruby on Rails with a postgreSQL database back-end and a React / Redux front-end. Amazon Web Service S3 allows for cloud-based hosting of files.

woofblr is a social media micro-blogging application that allows users to create various types of posts such as text and images. A dashboard displays content posted by other users that a user follows.
