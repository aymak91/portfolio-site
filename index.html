About Me

Hi! Thanks for checking out my page!
I'm Alex, a software engineer based in the SF Bay Area!

I received my bachelor's in Materials Engineering from UC San Diego and worked in a lab before moving to the San Francisco Bay Area to find new opportunities. Here I stumbled upon a large self driving car startup, where I played a large role in scaling a team of 5 to a testing fleet of about 100. I self taught myself some basic coding skills to create heatmap data visualizations that kept track of KPIs, which made me realize a passion for problen solving and building cool things and eventually inspired me to pursue software development through an intensive software engineering bootcamp. I have since continued to sharpen my skills in various languages, technologies, and frameworks.

I thrive in fast-changing environments and am no stranger to having to quickly respond to unexpected problems (which I often dub "dumpster fires").
