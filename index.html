About Alex

Hi! I'm Alex, a software engineer currently located in the SF Bay Area. Before software development, I was a project manager working in the autonomous vehicle industry. While working on projects there to improve work efficiency, I taught myself basic software development skills, which I enjoyed enough to push myself to pursue and complete App Academy's full stack software engineering program. There, I built skills in several technologies such as JavaScript, React / Redux, Ruby on Rails, Node.js, SQL, and the MERN stack.

When I'm not coding, I enjoying going on hikes with my puppy, PC building / gaming, and riding motorcycles on long twisty roads. Speaking of fast motorcycles...

I thrive in fast-changing environments and am no stranger to responding and solving new problems at a moment's notice. I am always seeking to work on cool projects with an awesome teams, and I would love to hear how I can make an impact on your organization!
